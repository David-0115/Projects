To Do List

HELP - Instructions

  1. Add Item to do list by keying item in 'Type here to add to do' box.
  2. Click 'Add To do, to add item to the list.
  3. When the To do is completed, click the green check button. If checked in error, click again.
  4. If you want to delete a To do, click the red X to remove it.
Note: List will be saved when you close your browser, so you can continue where you left off when you re-open the To Do List!
